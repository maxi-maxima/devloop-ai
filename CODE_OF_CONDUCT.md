# Code of Conduct

DevLoop AI follows the Contributor Covenant spirit: be respectful, collaborative, and practical.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.
- Welcome maintainers, developers, security practitioners, and AI tooling users with different levels of experience.
- Treat safety concerns seriously.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks,
- publishing private information,
- sharing secrets or private logs,
- submitting offensive tooling or unsafe exploit content,
- repeatedly ignoring maintainer safety guidance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject PRs; or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers listed in the repository.
